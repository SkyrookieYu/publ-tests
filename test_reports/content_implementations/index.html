Implementation Report for the Content Creator/Distributor Submissions.

The following content creators and distributors have used Publication Manifest or Audiobooks to create content for internal use or public/commercial distribution. Where possible, this content has been tested against some of the implementations mentioned in the Implementation Report. Any produced content that is released for testing can be found in the test_content folder in our GitHub repository. For distributors listed below, these organizations have either begun implementing or plan to implement shortly after the release of the Audiobooks specification, and intend to distribute and/or ingest Audiobooks content.

Please note that a content creator may be listed here and may not share their content publicly, all content creators listed will be confirmed by the chairs and editors of the Publishing Working Group.
